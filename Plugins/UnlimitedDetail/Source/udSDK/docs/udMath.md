# udMath

Provides math types and functions for udSDK, including vectors, quaternions, matrices, and common math utilities. All types are templated on element type T, with common typedefs provided for float and double variants. 

## Defines

### UD_PI

Pi (double precision). 

### UD_2PI

2\*Pi (double precision) 

### UD_HALF_PI

Pi/2 (double precision). 

### UD_ROOT_2

Square root of 2 (double precision). 

### UD_INV_ROOT_2

1/sqrt(2) (double precision) 

### UD_RAD2DEGVAL

Radians to degrees conversion factor (double precision). 

### UD_DEG2RADVAL

Degrees to radians conversion factor (double precision). 

### UD_RAD2DEG(rad)

Convert radians to degrees (double precision). 

### UD_DEG2RAD(deg)

Convert degrees to radians (double precision). 

### UD_PIf

Pi (single precision). 

### UD_2PIf

2\*Pi (single precision) 

### UD_HALF_PIf

Pi/2 (single precision). 

### UD_ROOT_2f

Square root of 2 (single precision). 

### UD_INV_ROOT_2f

1/sqrt(2) (single precision) 

### UD_RAD2DEGVALf

Radians to degrees conversion factor (single precision). 

### UD_DEG2RADVALf

Degrees to radians conversion factor (single precision). 

### UD_RAD2DEGf(rad)

Convert radians to degrees (single precision). 

### UD_DEG2RADf(deg)

Convert degrees to radians (single precision). 

### UD_EPSILON

Default epsilon for approximate comparisons.
